Tutorial - Step 6: Time Tool

The Time Tool keeps track of how time goes by on Mars and Earth. Here you can view the date on the Martian Calendar and slow down or speed up the passing of simulation time. You can also pause the simulation by clicking the Pause button, and resuming by clicking it again. Try selecting various speeds to see time pass faster or slower. You can for example look at how fast the inhabitants move in the other tools.
