Tutorial - Step 8: Preferences Tool

The Preferences Tool lets you control some overall settings in MSP. The controls should be more or less self-explanatory, but here is an overview:

The Mute button mutes MSP, but not your computers main sound output. The Volume slider just below lets you adjust MSP sound effect volume.

Native Look & Feel lets MSP use your operating systems built-in user interface design ('Look and Feel') instead of the one supplied with Java. This will give you a more integrated user experience, but may look odd in some cases.

Show Unit Toolbar and Show Toolbar shows or hides the respective bars, giving you a little more screen real estate if needed.
